Case study

StageWing — equipment rental marketplace

StageWing rents out equipment owned by other people and layers paid services on top. Two kinds of revenue with completely different costs were blended together, so the margins looked random and the founder couldn't tell what was actually making money. I separated the streams, rebuilt the forecast around each one, and added best-, base-, and worst-case toggles. Now every month starts with a clear answer: what's profitable, what isn't, and how long the cash lasts.
